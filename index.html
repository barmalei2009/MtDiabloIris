Who We Are

A Community United by Beauty

Based in the shadow of Mt. Diablo in Contra Costa County, we bring together gardeners, hybridizers, and nature lovers who share a passion for one of the world's most elegant flowers.

Whether you're a first-time grower or an experienced hybridizer, you'll find a welcoming community here — with knowledge to share and irises to admire.
