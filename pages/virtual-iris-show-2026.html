Virtual Iris Show 2026

Join us online for our Virtual Iris Show! Submit your best iris photos and celebrate the beauty of irises with our community from anywhere.

How to Participate

  1. Take high-quality photos of your irises in bloom (see image requirements).
  2. Email your photos to virtualshow@mtdiabloiris.org with your name, iris variety (if known), and location.
  3. Submissions accepted through April 24, 2026.
  4. All entries will be displayed in our online gallery and eligible for virtual awards.

Show Details

  • Open to all members and the public
  • Multiple entries allowed per person
  • Winners announced on April 27, 2026
  • Categories: Tall Bearded, Rebloomers, Arilbred, Novelty, and more

Questions?

Email virtualshow@mtdiabloiris.org for more information.
