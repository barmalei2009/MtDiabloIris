Who We Are

Mt. Diablo Iris Society Since 1967

The Mt. Diablo Iris Society is a community of gardeners, hybridizers, and iris enthusiasts dedicated to the appreciation, cultivation, and preservation of irises. We serve the greater Bay Area with educational programs, shows, and hands‑on learning.

Our Mission

  • Promote the growing and enjoyment of irises
  • Educate the public through programs, shows, and demonstrations
  • Support hybridizers and preserve historic iris varieties
  • Encourage community involvement and gardening education

Meetings

We meet monthly at the Lindsay Wildlife Experience in Walnut Creek. Meetings include guest speakers, culture tips, raffles, and opportunities to learn from experienced growers.

Membership

Membership is open to anyone with an interest in irises—beginners and experts alike. Members receive newsletters, access to plant sales, and opportunities to participate in shows and exhibitions.
