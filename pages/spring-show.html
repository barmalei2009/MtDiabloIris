Annual Event

Spring Show

Celebrate the season with our annual iris show featuring stunning blooms from members across the region. Enjoy competitive displays, expert judging, and a vibrant community atmosphere.

What to Expect

  • Hundreds of iris varieties on display
  • Judged competitions for all experience levels
  • Potted iris sales and club information
  • Educational exhibits and hands-on demonstrations
  • Family-friendly activities and refreshments

Location & Date

Venue: Alden Lane Nursery, 981 Alden Lane, Livermore, CA 94550
Date: See Events page for this year's show dates

How to Participate

  • Bring your best iris blooms for entry (all are welcome!)
  • Contact us for show schedules and entry guidelines
  • Volunteer opportunities available for members

Past Highlights

Our Spring Show has been a beloved tradition since 1961, drawing hundreds of entries and visitors each year. Join us to experience the beauty and camaraderie of the iris community!
